Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

1. Find a Printer: Locate a printer on campus that is connected to the university’s network. Printers are usually available in libraries, computer labs, and study areas.
2. Connect to the Network Printer: Ensure your laptop is connected to the HZ network. You may need to install a printer driver or use a university-provided printing service.
3. Print the Document: Open your document and select the “Print” option. Choose the network printer from the list of available printers. You may be required to authenticate with your student ID or login credentials to release the print job.

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

1. Locate a Scanner: Find a scanner available on campus, typically in libraries or administrative offices.
2. Use the Scanner: Place your document on the scanner bed and follow the on-screen instructions to scan it.
3. Send the Document: Choose the option to send the scanned document to your email or save it to a USB drive. You can then access it from your laptop.

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

1. Visit the HZ Web Shop: Go to the HZ web shop website.
2. Browse and Select Items: Browse through the available items or events and add them to your cart.
3. Checkout: Proceed to the checkout page, enter your payment details, and complete the purchase. You may need to log in with your student account or create an account if you don’t have one.

4. How can you book a project space?

1. Access the Booking System: Visit the HZ project space booking system website or application.
2. Select a Space: Choose a project space and date from the available options.
3. Book the Space: Follow the instructions to complete the booking. You may need to log in with your student credentials.

5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

1. Register Your Vehicle: Ensure that your vehicle is registered with the HZ parking office if required.
2. Find Parking: Locate the designated parking areas on the campus.
3. Follow Parking Rules: Adhere to the parking regulations and display any required permits or passes in your vehicle.
