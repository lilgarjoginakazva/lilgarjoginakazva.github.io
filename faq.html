Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

To print a document from your laptop at HZ, first locate a network-connected printer on campus. Then, select the "Print" option in your document, choose the network printer, and authenticate with your student ID or login credentials to release the print job.

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

To scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ, first locate a scanner on campus, usually in libraries or administrative offices. Place your document on the scanner bed and follow the on-screen instructions. Then, select the option to email the scanned document to yourself or save it to a USB drive.

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

To buy something on the HZ web shop, first visit the HZ web shop website. Browse the available items or events and add them to your cart. Then, proceed to checkout, enter your payment details, and complete the purchase. You may need to log in with your student account or create one if you don't have it.

4. How can you book a project space?

To book a project space, first access the HZ learn app. Select a space and date from the available options. Finally, follow the instructions to complete the booking, which may require logging in with your student credentials.

5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

To park your car at the HZ parking lot, first register your vehicle with the HZ parking office if required. Then, locate the designated parking areas on campus. Lastly, follow the parking rules and display any required permits or passes in your vehicle.
