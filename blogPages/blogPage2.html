Why I Want to Study ICT

I’ve always been drawn to the world of technology and how it shapes our daily lives. Studying ICT seems like a natural fit for me because it combines creativity with problem-solving, which I find super appealing. Programming allows me to build things from scratch and see my ideas come to life, whether that’s creating a cool app or just making a website look great.

While I dabble in 3D modeling and design, I appreciate how programming connects all aspects of technology. It’s not just about making things look good; it’s about how they function. Learning more about ICT will help me dive deeper into the tech world, giving me the skills to bring my creative visions to life while also ensuring everything runs smoothly behind the scenes. Overall, I’m excited to explore this field and see where it takes me!
